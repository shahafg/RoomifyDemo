Welcome to Roomify

A Project by Kfir Graidi and Shahaf Goren

Smart Room Management for Educational Institutions

Roomify is an innovative room management system designed specifically for educational institutions, streamlining the management of classes, rooms, and educational resources. Our platform provides an efficient solution for classroom management, lesson coordination, and activity planning in an organized and intuitive manner.

The platform empowers teachers, students, and administrative staff to plan and execute activities while optimizing the use of rooms and additional resources such as technological equipment, computers, and interactive boards, thereby enhancing the learning and management experience.
